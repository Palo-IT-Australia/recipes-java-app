Installer guides

The App 'Filter' allows you to search by patient, and sort by alpha, date or time period.
 
Access all patient reports and images linked to you. Refine your search using the 'Filter' icon.
 
Use the 'Action' button then the 'Arrow' button to click through an image series.
 
Scroll through images, use two finger "pinch" to zoom or shrink screen, and two fingers to drag image around screen.
