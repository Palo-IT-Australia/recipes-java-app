I-MED Online 2.0 for medical practitioners

Welcome to I-MED Online 2.0, where practitioners can quickly and easily access patient images and reports, on desktop, tablet or mobile. From this page practitioners can also order referral pads and ask for technical support.
Results - access patient images and reports

I-MED Online 2.0 gives practitioners access to diagnostic images and reports via desktop and mobile. We are progressively rolling out I-MED Online 2.0 across the country.
Request imaging via our online e-Referral

Our easy-to-use online imaging request is available to our I-MED Online 2.0 users. It is simple to use, compatible with desktop, mobile and tablet.
e-Referrals for better patient outcomes

Send us your imaging requests electronically and securely, via HealthLink, from within your Best Practice software. We'll contact your patient to make an appointment.
Support

Should you need support with the new E-Referral, with I-MED Online 2.0 or any other issue, help is at hand. Our support team is here to assist by phone, chat or email.
